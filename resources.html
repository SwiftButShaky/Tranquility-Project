Find Support and Guidance

If you or someone you know is struggling with mental health, help is available. Here are some key resources:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: Call or text 988 for confidential support.
  • Therapy and Counseling Services: Find a licensed therapist through Psychology Today.
  • Support Groups: Join mental health communities like NAMI for peer support.
  • Online Mental Health Platforms: Websites such as BetterHelp offer online counseling.
  • Mental Health Education: Visit MentalHealth.gov for awareness and information.
